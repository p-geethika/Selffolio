Face Recognition based Attendance System

This repo consists of face recognition based attendance system which takes our in attendance and out attendance in an easy way. Faceroll face attendance works by verifying your face against the enrolled face to mark your daily attendance. All you need to do is stand in front of the camera and your face is verified instantly in milliseconds. The solution works on most android devices so you don't need a separate hardware device to use faceroll.
